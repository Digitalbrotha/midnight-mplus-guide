⚠️ MOST COMMON MISTAKE
Ascendant Lord - Luminous Beam
The beam looks dangerous, but you MUST stand IN it. Standing outside causes massive damage. This is backwards from typical mechanics and causes most wipes.
Position INSIDE the beam path. This is counterintuitive but required. Coordinate with healer to ensure coverage.
  • Memorize beam width and positioning angle
  • Don't panic when standing in the visual effect
  • Maintain damage uptime while in beam
  • Call out beam phases to group

Feather Storm (Windrunner Spire)
Ground AoE that travels across room. Maintain DPS while continuously moving. Stutter-step to keep uptime.
Practice stutter-stepping: target stays visible while moving. This maintains DPS uptime during mechanics.
  • Plan your movement path in advance
  • Use pillars/obstacles for LOS and pathing
  • Stutter-step rather than full sprint
  • Maintain range from melee to avoid double mechanics

Void Nova & Knockbacks
Around-the-boss AoE with knockback. Ranged recover faster due to distance. Position near walls if possible.
Your range is an advantage here! You recover quicker and can get back to DPS faster than melee. Position strategically.
  • Position 25+ yards away to take reduced knockback
  • Have backs to wall if possible (prevents extra knockback)
  • Ready your resume-DPS ability immediately after knockback
  • Account for momentum when predicting landing zone
Leap Attack (Cruelfang)
Boss jumps to random ranged player. Spread out. When targeted, run in predetermined direction.
This is YOUR responsibility to handle. Know your escape route before pull. Keep moving post-leap to avoid follow-up attacks.
  • Assign escape routes per ranged player position
  • Don't run toward healer or other mechanics
  • Be spread minimum 8 yards apart
  • Continue DPS immediately after leap resolves
⚠️ POSITIONING CRITICAL
Frost Bolt (Selandria)
Spell bounces between nearby players. Max spread is essential. Spread reduces number of bounces dramatically.

Player Position
You have the advantage here! Use your 25+ yard range to stay spread. Prevents bounces from hitting group.
Blizzard (Selandria) - Pre-Positioning
Room fills with AoE hazard. Must pre-position in safe zones before cast starts. Can't escape once started.
Position in predetermined zones BEFORE boss casts. Watch for cast telegraph and move immediately to safe area.
  • Learn safe zone locations beforehand
  • Pre-position 3-5 seconds before expected cast
  • Don't wait for visual confirmation of cast
  • Coordinate safe zones with group to prevent congestion
