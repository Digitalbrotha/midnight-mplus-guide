## 🎯 Most Important Mechanics to Master

### Priority 1 (Will wipe your group)
- **Luminous Beam** - Must stand IN the beam (counter-intuitive!)
- **Static Charge** - 8+ yard spread mandatory
- **Frost Bolt** - Maximum spread required
- **Blizzard** - Pre-position BEFORE cast starts

### Priority 2 (Common mistakes)
- **Feather Storm** - Stutter-stepping required
- **Leap Attack** - Know escape route beforehand
- **Portal Summon** - Coordinate add priority

### Priority 3 (Optimization)
- **Knockback Recovery** - Position near walls
- **Phase Management** - Understand transitions
- **Add Cleaving** - Balance adds + boss DPS

## 💡 Top Tips

1. **Always look for the ⚠️ symbol** - Counter-intuitive mechanics that kill unprepared groups
2. **Pre-position before mechanics happen** - Many mechanics require being in position 3-5 seconds before they occur
3. **Communicate proactively** - Call out mechanics as you see them
4. **Master stutter-stepping early** - This single technique increases your DPS uptime by 20-30%
5. **Track your own debuffs** - Don't wait for healer to call them out
6. **Spread is your friend** - Use your range advantage; be the first to spread
7. **Practice solo first** - Learn positions in Mythic 0 before pushing keys
8. **Admit mistakes quickly** - This helps group learn and adjust

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Files won't open in browser"
- Make sure you're opening .html files (not viewing in text editor)
- Right-click → Open with → Your browser
- Or drag .html file into browser window

### "Filters not working in index.html"
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Try refreshing the page
- Try a different browser

### "Visuals look blurry or wrong"
- Try another browser (Chrome/Firefox/Edge recommended)
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Check your screen zoom level (should be 100%)

### "Cheatsheet doesn't look right on print"
- Open in Firefox or Chrome (best print support)
- Use "Print to PDF" for digital copies
- Set margins to 0.5" for best results
- Use "Fit to page" settings
