Ranged DPS Cheat Sheet | All 8 Dungeons

🎯 Positioning Fundamentals
  • Distance: 25+ yards from boss
  • Positioning: Flank (90°) or back (180°)
  • Spread: 8+ yards from other DPS
  • Optimal: Where you can see adds + healer
  • Never: Stand in front of boss
⚠️ Most Common Wipes
  • Luminous Beam: STAND IN BEAM (not outside!)
  • Static Charge: 8+ yard spread mandatory
  • Frost Bolt: Max spread to prevent bounces
  • Blizzard: Pre-position BEFORE cast

✨ Your Advantages
  • Max reaction time (25+ yards)
  • No melee cleave damage
  • Can spread easily
  • See entire arena
  • Faster KB recovery
🚶 Stutter-Stepping
Why: Maintain DPS during movement
How:
  1. Press move key (0.1-0.2 sec)
  2. Release key
  3. Attack once
  4. Repeat
Practice: Dummy training
📍 Debuff Management
  • Track your debuff stack count
  • Call out at 4+ stacks
  • Move away for cleanse
  • Plan spread before debuff
  • Use arena geometry to escape
🔄 Phase Management
  • High Damage: Play safe, maintain DPS
  • Add Phase: Switch to priority immediately
  • Transition: Prepare for next phase
  • Burst Window: Coordinate group CDs
📢 What To Callout
  • "Spread" when debuff incoming
  • Debuff count when 4+ stacks
  • Add locations as they spawn
  • Mechanic names as you see them
  • "Watch [mechanic]" for new players
🚨 Emergency Callouts
  • "SPREAD NOW" - debuff incoming
  • "STOP DPS" - burst will trigger bad
  • "TANK NEEDS HELP" - clear adds
  • "REPOSITION" - change zones
