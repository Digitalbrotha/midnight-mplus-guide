## 🎯 Most Important Mechanics for Ranged DPS

### Tier 1: This Will Wipe Your Group If You Fail

1. **Luminous Beam (Nexus-Point Xenas - Ascendant Lord)**
   - **Problem:** Mechanic is counter-intuitive. Players MUST stand IN the beam.
   - **Why it kills:** Players assume beam is dangerous and run away, causing massive group damage.
   - **Solution:** Memorize this before encounter. Tell your team about this ahead of time.
   - **Your Role:** Lead the positioning. Be the example for correct positioning.

2. **Static Charge (Windrunner Spire - Talon King)**
   - **Problem:** Debuff jumps between nearby players if they stand too close.
   - **Why it kills:** Entire group stacked for healer, debuff spreads to everyone.
   - **Solution:** Maintain 8+ yards from all players. Use your range advantage.
   - **Your Role:** You can be further away than melee. Set the spread pattern.

3. **Feather Storm (Windrunner Spire - Talon King)**
   - **Problem:** Ground AoE travels across room. Must maintain DPS uptime while moving.
   - **Why it kills:** Players either get hit (no movement) or stop DPS (too much moving).
   - **Solution:** Stutter-step. Practice keeping target visible while moving.
   - **Your Role:** Master stutter-stepping. Help tank maintain positioning.

4. **Void Nova & Portal Summon (Seat of the Triumvirate)**
   - **Problem:** Multi-mechanic phase with adds spawning during knockback effects.
   - **Why it kills:** Chaos if group isn't coordinated. Adds not burned fast enough.
   - **Solution:** Have priority targets marked. Coordinate burst phases.
   - **Your Role:** Cleave to adds while maintaining mobility for positioning.

### Tier 2: Common Mistakes

1. **Frost Bolt (Selandria - Magisters' Terrace)**
   - Bounces between players. Spread is ESSENTIAL. Don't stand near other DPS.

2. **Leap Attack (Cruelfang - Pit of Saron)**
   - Boss jumps to ranged players. You must have predetermined escape routes.
   - Communicate jumps to your group immediately.

3. **Blizzard (Selandria - Magisters' Terrace)**
   - Must pre-position before cast starts. Can't escape mid-AoE.
   - PRE-POSITION 3-5 seconds before expected cast time.

## 🔧 Ranged DPS Positioning Principles

### General Rules

**Optimal Distance:** 25+ yards from boss
- Gives you maximum reaction time
- Allows recovery from knockbacks
- Prevents cleave damage
- Maintains LOS to healer

**Positioning Zones:**
1. **Flanking (Best):** 90° to boss facing direction. Can see adds, boss mechanics, and healer.
2. **Back Angle (Good):** 180° from boss front. Must be careful not to block healer.
3. **Front (Bad):** Never stand in front of boss. Most mechanics are front-cleave.

**Safe Spread Formation:**
- Stand at max range corners of arena
- Maintain 8+ yard minimum from other DPS
- Don't stand behind pillars/walls unless mechanics require LoS

## ⚡ Advanced Ranged DPS Tips

### Stutter-Stepping Optimization
Stutter-stepping = short movement + immediate stop for one attack
- **How:** Press move key briefly (0.1-0.2 sec), release, attack
- **Why:** Maintains DPS uptime during movement phases
- **Practice:** Dummy training, start slow, increase speed

### Cleave Efficiency
When adds must be burned:
1. Position where you can see both boss and adds
2. Use AoE abilities when adds are near boss
3. Single-target priority targets when separated
4. Help other DPS focus priority targets

### Debuff Tracking
- Keep mental count of your own debuffs
- Call out when you reach critical stack counts
- Plan movement before debuff expires
- Coordinate stun/cleanse order with healer

### Boss Mechanics Priority
Learn the phases:
1. High-damage phases = position safely, maintain DPS
2. Add phases = switch to priority targets immediately
3. Transition phases = prepare for next mechanic
4. Burst phases = coordinate group DPS windows

## 🚨 Common Wipe Scenarios & How to Avoid Them

### Scenario 1: "Everyone Stacks for Heal"
**Problem:** Full group standing together, debuff spreads to everyone
**Solution:** Listen for "spread" call. Ranged LEAD the spreading by moving first.

### Scenario 2: "We're All Bunched During Knockback"
**Problem:** Knockback sends everyone into walls/hazards at once
**Solution:** Position spread BEFORE knockback mechanic. Have coordinated landing zones.

### Scenario 3: "Adds Not Burned Fast Enough"
**Problem:** Group focused on boss, adds alive too long
**Solution:** Have DPS assignments. Ranged can help with cleave from distance.

### Scenario 4: "Running From Beam Instead of Into It"
**Problem:** Luminous beam fails catastrophically
**Solution:** Pre-brief the group. Explain the mechanic. Go first as example.

### Scenario 5: "Healer Gets Hit by Jump Attack"
**Problem:** Ranged player doesn't escape properly, healer stands too close
**Solution:** Have predetermined escape routes. Don't escape TOWARD healer.
